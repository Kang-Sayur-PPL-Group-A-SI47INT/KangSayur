# SPRINT #1 Work Plan - KANG SAYUR

## Objective
The primary goal for this sprint is to build a **MVP marketplace** where users can browse products and prepare orders.

By the end of this sprint, the platform should function as a browsable marketplace where farmers can manage their listings and consumers can view details, and add items to their carts.
